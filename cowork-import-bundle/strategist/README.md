# Strategist Pack

9 skills that give you a full strategy team inside Claude.

## What's inside

| ZIP | What it does |
|---|---|
| `strategy-architect.zip` | Designs strategy. Picks the big choices. |
| `strategy-change.zip` | Spots the "people stuff" that kills good plans. |
| `strategy-competitive.zip` | Stress-tests your strategy against what competitors will do. |
| `strategy-engagement.zip` | Helps you run workshops and find what stakeholders actually need. |
| `strategy-finance.zip` | Kills weak business cases. Makes sure the numbers add up. |
| `strategy-integrity.zip` | Questions whether your process is real or just for show. |
| `strategy-intelligence.zip` | Pulls market and customer signals into your strategy. |
| `strategy-monitoring.zip` | Builds KPIs and scorecards that actually help you decide. |
| `strategy-operationalization.zip` | Turns strategy into weekly routines people can stick to. |

## How to install

See **START-HERE.md** in the parent folder.
